Whitewater Adventures Site Plan

Adam Humes

Overview

Purpose

We want to attract old an new customers to engage and use our whitewater rafting services. Customers can have any experience level. We will rent out equipment and guides to help them on their way to enjoying whitewater rapids.

Audience

Who are the target Customers? The audience will range from scout troops looking for a group activity, to business retreat groups, to thrill seekers. What do they expect to find on the webstie? On the website, they should expect a run-down of the experience to help them understand how the activity will go. They should find answers to pricing and locations. How will my audience usually access my site? The audience will most likely be finding the site either on a laptop or mobile device.
